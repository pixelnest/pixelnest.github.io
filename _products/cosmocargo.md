**Cosmo Cargo** is an inventory autobattler roguelite game where you fight to preserve the chaos of the galaxy. Collect and combine items in your ship to gain powerful effects and defeat the Administrators.

## Explore the Galaxy

The galaxy is in danger! The terrible Administrators are on the march to organize the universe in their own way and destroy everything they consider disorderly.

But it’s not over yet! A rebel force is organizing to repel them. And you're part of it!

Travel through different sectors and meet amazing alien peoples who may be able to help you in your fight! 

## Collect items & equip your ship

Objects are key! Buy, trade, find and steal all kinds of crazy items with powerful effects.
Combine them in your ship to create new synergies, powerful chain reactions, and satisfying combos.
Upgrade objects by collecting copies to unlock new effects.
Expand your inventory by unlocking cluttered slots for even more possibilities.

## Expand your crew

Traveling alone is no fun, and there's room in the ship's cockpit. So why not recruit some new crew members?

These new characters can use their unique powers in conjunction with the ship's equipment for even more combos.

## Take on the Administration

The Administration aims to destroy disorder in the universe. Join the Rebels and help them defeat the minions of this terrible organization.

Take part in epic space battles, inventory against inventory. Face increasingly tough (and weird) enemies thanks to items collected in the sector.

And once the minions have been defeated, it's the Chief Administrator who must be vanquished, so be prepared!
