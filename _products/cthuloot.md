**CTHULOOT** is a coop arcade game for up to 4 players planned for PC and consoles.

CTHULOOT is a cooperative arcade game in which you explore strange places filled with treasures to collect. Cooperation, agility and cthonic chanting will be required to solve the mysteries that await you. But watch out for tentacles! 

THE GREAT OLD ONES ARE CALLING!
Round up your friends to form a gang of semi-competent looters in search of the relics lost by the Great Old Ones. Just be careful not to invoke them again!

COOP MULTIPLAYER GAME TO PLAY WITH YOUR FRIENDS
Explore mysterious realms in the liminal space between reality and madness, and collect mad loot along the way!

FIND AND COLLECT ANCESTRAL RELICS
Help Lord Morgaz'han regain his rightful place among the Great Old Ones with the power of the treasures you'll find. Draw on his collection of supernatural watches to acquire new skills! 

WILL YOU STILL BE FRIENDS WHEN IT'S ALL OVER?
Show off your skills as a looter, and don't lose anything along the way if you don't want to drag your team down!
